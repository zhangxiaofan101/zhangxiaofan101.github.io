Xiaofan Zhang

Associate Professor · Qing Yuan Research Institute , Shanghai Jiao Tong University

Hi! I am an Associate Professor at Qing Yuan Research Institute , SJTU. Prior to joining SJTU, I spent two years at SenseBrain (New Jersey) and one year at JD Group Silicon Valley Research Center.

I obtained my B.E. from Beihang University and my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the College of Computing and Informatics, UNC Charlotte.

My research interests include pathology image analysis, LLMs in the medical domain, and multi-modality decision making.

I am recruiting students. If you have a purposeless curiosity about this world, feel free to contact me! ✉
